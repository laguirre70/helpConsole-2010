Logbook Reminders

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

Logbook Reminders are usually action items that a Staff Member needs to follow up on in order for a task to be completed.

Logbook Reminders:

Select the logbook entry that you would like to send the reminder about.  Open that entry.

There are two things that need to be done:

1.       In the note field state why you are sending a reminder.

2.      Then Click the Button Reminder

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 



After you click the reminder button a new box will come up:



 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 


To Send the Message you need three things:

1.      Date and time that you want to be reminded.

2.      Who you are sending it to – single click on the names or department

3.      Then strike Send

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

These Reminders will pop up on the date and time they were programed to pop up and you will see them on your toolbar once if you hit the remind me again button.
