Searching Contacts

To find a specific contact, click on the Search tab and enter the first 3 or 4 letters into the "Primary Name" field and click Search.



You can also locate contacts with a more general search by using the "Keyword" if you know the function the contact performs or services they offer such as Restaurant or Florist.


All contacts may be opened by clicking directly on the contact or by clicking on the "Edit" on the right of the screen
