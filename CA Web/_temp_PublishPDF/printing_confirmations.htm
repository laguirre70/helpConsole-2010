Printing Confirmations

Confirmations can be printed in 2 ways. The first is simply clicking on the “Print Confirmation” text in the Logbook main screen display as shown below.

Another option is to print the Confirmation directly from the logbook entry itself, as displayed below. Notice that there are other print options available in the drop down as well.



Click on the Print button




The confirmation will be displayed. Click on the printer for the final product.
