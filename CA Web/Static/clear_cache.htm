Clear Cache

To clear your Internet Explorer version 8 (IE8) browser cache simply follow these steps:

1.    Press the "ALT" button on your keyboard.
2.    Click on the "TOOLS" menu.
3.    In the drop down menu, locate and click on "Delete Browsing History." This will open another window titled "Delete Browsing History."
4.    In the "Delete Browsing History" window, locate and select the "Temporary Internet Files."
5.    Look to the bottom of the "Delete Browsing History" window, locate and click on the "Delete" box.
6.    Wait for the computer to tell you the files have been deleted. Click on the "Okay" button when you see the "Internet Options" box.
