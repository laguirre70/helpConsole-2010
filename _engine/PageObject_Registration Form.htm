Collect Data with a Registration Form
This is an example of a 'Registration Form' that can be added to your help system, or website to collect prospect or customer information. When you log in as 'Admin', you'll see a list of registration forms that have been submitted. Registration forms could be implemented for any of the following:
- Download a software trial
- Download a PDF manual
- Download a software patch
- Register for an upcoming seminar
- Instructor Evaluation
- Customer Survey
- Warranty Registration

Redirect URL
You can specify a 'redirect URL' so that when a form is submitted, the user will be redirected to a download file, a thank you page, etc.

Email Notification
This example doesn't send any email notifications but you can configure your registration form to send an email to a predefined email address when forms are submitted

Fully Customizable
The detail form and find form are fully customizable. You can add any number of text, date, number, checkbox, memo and list fields. Fields can also be marked as required.
