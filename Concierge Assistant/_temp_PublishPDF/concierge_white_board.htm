Concierge White Board

The Concierge Whiteboard is there to help you communicate digitally what you used to write on the whiteboard in the back office.



To maintain the whiteboard you simply double-click on the line number you wish to change. Key in the new text, select your color and check the check box if you wish to use the "bold" font. Then press enter or click on the "Save" button.
