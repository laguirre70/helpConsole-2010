PCI PA-DSS

Visa CISP

As of December 2006, The Concierge Assistant® is a Visa CISP and PABP validated solution. CA uses Triple DES/AES methods to encrypt credit card information. As part of the Visa CISP validation, below are 2 document links that you may find helpful;

1. Best Practices - Credit Card Encryption which can be used as a training document for your staff members, specifically for those that are authorized to de-encrypt the credit card.

2. The CA Visa CISP Implementation Guide which gives a comprehensive overview of the CISP program as it relates to The Concierge Assistant.
